Health Reset

Current State — live daemon backoff/degradation status.   Recent History — last error, fallback, and shutdown context.   Historical Counters — cumulative evidence, preserved by default.

Current State

Clears active degradation and backoff state. Use after resolving a transient brain issue.

Recent History

Clears last error, fallback, and shutdown fields. Historical counters remain.

Current + Recent recommended

Standard baseline reset. Historical counters remain untouched — safe for routine use.

Advanced

Historical Counter Resets

Use only when intentionally clearing cumulative evidence. These actions are more destructive and still fully audited. Historical counters represent long-term evidence of auth failures, brain fallbacks, and other health signals — clear with care.
